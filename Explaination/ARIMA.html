Time Series(ARIMA Model)

ARIMA stands for autoregressive integrated moving average

  1. Usally consider the frequency (時段/季度) and time
  2. consider the change in frequency (時段/季度) and time to predict the future
  3. Include two model
    • with frequency (時段/季度): ARIMA(P,D,Q)s
    • withOut frequency (時段/季度): ARIMA(p, d, q)
  4. We need to consider the following four paramters
    • p:find out the value in the past affect the value now.
      ex: the temperature in the past three days are 20 degrees .
      we can predict the temperature is 20 degree today.
    • d:The change in the past
      ex: the temperature increase rapidly in last three days.
      the probability of temperature increase today is high.
    • q:We can set the error rate equal to the difference we observed in the past
